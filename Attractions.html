Popular Attractions

Yala National Park

Want to get up close and personal with the untamed world of wildlife? then a visit to Yala national park is just for you. Whether you are in love with nature or visiting a park for the first time, you are guaranteed to have new experiences and appreciation towards nature.


Being the home to 44 varieties of mammals and 215 bird species, and also the largest concentration of leopards in the world. you wont run out of sights to see.



Sigiriya

Dubbed the 8th wonder of the world, this ancient rock fortress is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Sri lanka. from the beautiful frescoes to the fountains experience the genius urban architecture while exploring the ancient ruins of this Capital



Arugambay

Who doesn't love a relaxing beach? well arugambay beach will take away your stress. from the calming breeze to the deep blue ocean experience the island nation in all its glory in one of the popular surf spots in the country.
